I am on the executive board of the McGill Graduate Association of Physics Students (MGAPS), where I've helped organize several events. I am also a physics delegate (one of three) to AGSEM, the union for teaching assistants and graduate employees at McGill.

At AGSEM, in addition to being the physics representative, I am on the constitutional review committee for the academic year 2018-2019, making sure the Union documents (the Constitution and the Policy Manual) are kept up to date in language and content. As of 2018-2019, I welcome suggestions/questions regarding these documents at sreela.das@nyu.edu, however this is not the official email, and official requests/grievances should always be sent to physics.delegate@agsem-aeedem.ca

I've also been part of the mentorship programs at NYU and McGill, and am involved with the Women in Physics group at McGill.
