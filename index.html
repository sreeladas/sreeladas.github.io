Sreela Das

Sreela is currently a second year MSc student at McGill University's VERITAS group where she works with Prof. Ken Ragan.

She did her undergraduate degree at New York University's Department of Physics  where she worked with Prof. Andy Haas at the NYU Department of Physics on the seach for millicharged particles at the LHC.

She also worked with Prof. Martijn Cloos at the NYU Langone Medical Center on MR Fingerprinting and Image reconstruction.
